Delete Your BioQvant Account & Data

This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and all associated data for the BioQvant app (developer: BioQvant).

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at bioqvant.support@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Include your username and a clear request for account and data deletion.
  3. We will confirm your identity and process your request within 7 days.

What Data Is Deleted?

  • Your account information (username, email, phone, etc.)
  • All data you have stored in the BioQvant app

What Data Is Retained?

  • None, unless required by law (e.g., for fraud prevention or legal compliance)

Retention Period

  • All your data will be deleted within 7 days of your verified request.

If you have any questions, contact us at bioqvant.support@gmail.com.
